SETUPS TO IMPLEMENT (minimum viable):
Setup A — Thu–Fri–Mon Magnet
	•	Trigger evaluated after Friday close:
Condition: High(Fri) < High(Thu)
Magnet: Low(Fri)
Target day: next trading day after Friday (normally Monday)
Hit definition: on target day RTH, intraday Low <= Magnet

Setup B — Mon–Wed–Thu Magnet
	•	Trigger evaluated after Wednesday close:
Condition: High(Wed) < High(Mon)
Magnet: Low(Wed)
Target day: next trading day after Wednesday (normally Thursday)
Hit definition: on target day RTH, intraday Low <= Magnet

Setup C — Gap Fill
	•	Trigger evaluated using today open vs prior close:
Gap% = (Open(today) - Close(prevDay)) / Close(prevDay)
If abs(Gap%) >= gap_threshold (default 0.30%):
Magnet: Close(prevDay)
Target day: same day
Hit definition: during RTH, price touches magnet:
- gap up: intraday Low <= magnet
- gap down: intraday High >= magnet

Setup D — Inside Day Expansion / Sweep Watch
	•	Trigger evaluated after daily close:
Condition: High(today) < High(yesterday) AND Low(today) > Low(yesterday)
Magnets: Yesterday High and Yesterday Low
Target day: next trading day
Validation: During target day RTH, record:
	•	touched_high (intraday High >= PDH)
	•	touched_low (intraday Low <= PDL)
	•	which_touched_first (timestamp ordering if both touched)

Always-On Reference (toggleable):
Module E — PDH/PDL Sweep Watch
	•	Every day: PDH and PDL become magnets for next day.
	•	Validation next day RTH as above.
	•	Record first-touch ordering.

Optional (toggleable):
Module F — Weak Extreme Proxy (revisit within 1–3 days)
	•	Trigger after daily close:
If day’s Low is within 0.05% of a “mechanical level” (choose any of: PDL, round .00/.50, or user-defined level list)
AND the day closes in the top 35% of its daily range,
Then mark Low as a weak-extreme magnet for next 3 trading days.
	•	Validation: within next 1–3 trading days RTH, intraday Low <= that Low.
